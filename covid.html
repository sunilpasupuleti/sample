CovidFYI


It is a digital tool to fight COVID-19, and also misinformation about the pandemic. covidfyi.in, is a one-stop digital directory for all COVID-19 related emergency services from government agencies.

Project Overview


A digital tool which aims to bring the right information from the from the right sources to the right people. ,only official information from government organisation is put on this platform to ensure authenticity and credibility




Problem Context


Mass media and social networks have played important role in disseminating information regarding public health.


During COVID-19 pandemic, misinformation and fake news have represented an important issue generating confusion and insecurity among the population




Solution


covidFYI is a platform for citizens, government organisations/ institutions alike and provides authenticated information on authorised laboratories near you.


Also provides information on designated hospitals, grocery stores, mental health helpline, emergency helplines, field officers contacts, emergency doctors to call, fever clinics near you.


My role was to design the layout of the tabs, it included in the design of the information architecture in the website with a visual UI cards.
