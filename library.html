Library management system


It is a Web-application for students and librarian where students gives feedback on availability of the required books and contains student profile.



My Role

Design and develop the application


Team mates

Individual project


Tools used

HTML, CSS, JSP, Oracle database





Project Overview


It is a project that acts as an interface to the students and library in knowing their needs, complaints and requests




Problem Context


Student sometimes faces issues with the books that are old or need some new ones. Some other are


  • Student wants to give feedback about library
  • Student request for new books and magazines
  • Maintenance of student log in and log out timings
  • Maintenance of student due’s of his books



Solution


The solution is bringing up an interface that helps students and library to communicate regarding these Issues called Library Management system


Library Management System maintains the student records, requests, complaints about the library and effectively manages them with an Administrator


Admin role is generally taken up by the Librarian or Assistant Librarian.


Faculty could also have the same benefits as a student in the interface




Design


By taking account on all the pain points that students face in the library, have laid out Information Architecture of the website.




Information architecture



Data flow diagram


The two major users of the website are student and the Admin; who is either the Librarian or the Assistant librarian.


Student module


Student registers himself using his details and logs into the account





Student logs into the account and fills up the form to post his request or complaint details.

Student also specifies his In time and out time in the form.






Admin module


Admin registers himself using his details and logs into the account to check out the student report and In time, out time of the students, also the feedback of him.





Admin generates report when he logs into the account.






Contact details


Contact details are provided for the students in the website for any queries at higher management level
